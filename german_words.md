💡 Quick Tips for German Articles:
	∙	die is most common — used for most abstract nouns, -ung, -heit, -keit, -schaft, -ion endings
	∙	das is often used for diminutives (-chen, -lein) and many neuter concepts
	∙	der is common for days, months, seasons, and many masculine roles
	∙	When in doubt, die is statistically the most frequent article in German!​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​
